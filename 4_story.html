THE STORY

The Fall of the Van der Linde Gang

The End of an Era

America, 1899. The end of the Wild West era has begun. After a robbery goes badly wrong in the western town of Blackwater, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee. With federal agents and the best bounty hunters in the nation massing on their heels, the gang must rob, steal and fight their way across the rugged heartland of America in order to survive.

As deepening internal divisions threaten to tear the gang apart, Arthur must make a choice between his own ideals and loyalty to the gang who raised him.

Major Chapters

Chapter 1: Colter

The gang escapes through the mountains in a blizzard after the Blackwater massacre.

Chapter 2: Horseshoe Overlook

The gang sets up camp in Valentine and starts rebuilding their resources.

Chapter 3: Clemens Point

Moving to Rhodes, tensions rise as the gang gets caught between feuding families.

Chapter 4: Saint Denis

The gang attempts a big score in the city, but things spiral out of control.

Chapter 5: Guarma

Shipwrecked on a Caribbean island, the gang fights to survive and escape.

Chapter 6: Beaver Hollow

Back in America, the gang falls apart as betrayals come to light.

Themes

Red Dead Redemption 2 explores themes of loyalty, morality, and the cost of violence. As Arthur Morgan confronts his own mortality, he must reckon with the choices he's made and the life he's lived. The story questions what it means to be an outlaw when the world is changing, and whether redemption is possible for those who've lived by the gun.
