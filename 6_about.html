The Making of a Masterpiece

The Game

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a 2018 action-adventure game developed and published by Rockstar Games. The game is the third entry in the Red Dead series and a prequel to the 2010 game Red Dead Redemption. Set in 1899 in a fictionalized representation of the Western, Midwestern, and Southern United States, the story follows outlaw Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang.

The game is presented through first and third-person perspectives, and the player may freely roam its interactive open world. Gameplay elements include shootouts, heists, hunting, horseback riding, interacting with non-player characters, and maintaining the character's honor rating through moral choices and deeds.

Development

Red Dead Redemption 2 was developed by Rockstar Studios, a collaborative team of developers spread across several Rockstar Games studios worldwide. The game's development lasted over eight years, beginning soon after Red Dead Redemption's release, and became one of the most expensive video games ever made.

  • 8+ years in development
  • Over 2,000 developers involved
  • 300,000+ animations created
  • 500,000+ lines of dialogue recorded
  • 1,200 actors and crew members

Critical Acclaim

Red Dead Redemption 2 received widespread critical acclaim, with praise directed at its story, characters, open world, graphics, music, and level of detail. It won numerous year-end accolades including Game of the Year awards from several gaming publications and is considered one of the greatest video games ever made.
